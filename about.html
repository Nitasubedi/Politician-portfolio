Prakriti Dhakal

"Dedicated to transparent leadership, public service and inclusive development."

Prakriti Dhakal is a passionate young political leader committed to public service, good governance and community empowerment. She believes in transparent leadership, inclusive development and meaningful citizen participation.

She actively works with youth, women and local communities to create positive social change through policy discussions, education, leadership programs and public engagement.

Her political journey is driven by honesty, accountability and a strong commitment to building a better future for every citizen.
